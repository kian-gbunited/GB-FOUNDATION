About Us

Our Story

Why the Foundation Exists

GB United Foundation was created to close a real gap. Too many children in Los Angeles have no access to organized sports, quality coaching, or the sense of belonging that comes from being on a team.

Soccer is the vehicle. Confidence, discipline, and opportunity for kids who've had too little of all three.

We work in underserved neighborhoods and public schools across LA — coaching, gear, and community for families who can't afford club sports.

Vision, Mission & Focus

What We're Building

👁️

Vision

A Los Angeles where no child is left out of sport because of where they live or what their family can afford.

🚀

Mission

To unite and uplift our community by providing equitable access to soccer, fostering confidence, teamwork, and personal development in every child we serve.

🎯

Focus

Underserved children and public schools across LA — building skills alongside inclusion, confidence, and belonging.
