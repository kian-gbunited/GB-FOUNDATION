Our Story

Why the Foundation Exists

GB United Foundation was created to address a gap in the community. Soccer is the vehicle — the real impact is confidence, belonging, discipline, and opportunity for kids who've had too little of all four.

We work in underserved neighborhoods and public schools across Los Angeles, bringing coaching, gear, and community to children whose families can't afford club sports.

Every child, regardless of zip code or income, deserves a coach who believes in them.

Vision

A Los Angeles where no child is left out of sport because of where they live or what their family can afford.

🚀

Mission

To unite and uplift our community by providing equitable access to soccer, fostering confidence, teamwork, and personal development in every child we serve.

🎯

Target

Underserved children and public schools throughout Los Angeles — building athletic skills alongside inclusion, confidence, and growth.
